Our Mission

FH HOPE aims to empower people in the community with a better understanding of STEM concepts by running workshops so that they can make an impact on the lives of people around the world.

However, our empowerment doesn't stop there. Our empowerment of people extends to providing humanitarian outreach and support to people around the world, giving the less fortunate a better chance of breaking the barriers that prevent them from reaching their full potential and making an impact on the world.

Currently, we are providing this humanitarian outreach by fundraising to provide equal education to children around the world which enables them to see better and work towards their goals. We hope to fundraise for many more organizations this year.
